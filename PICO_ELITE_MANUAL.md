# 💠 PICO ELITE: Official Operations Manual

Welcome to **Pico Elite**, the ultimate DSA training grounds. This ecosystem is designed for high-performance learning with an "Extreme Gaming" edge. Below are your primary command sectors.

---

### 🏛️ [HQ] The Mission Dashboard
*Your central command for topic mastery.*
- **Subject Bar**: Located at the top. Whichever subject you study last automatically moves to the **#1 spot** (Duolingo Style).
- **Stages**: The curriculum is broken into Stages. Click **💥 INITIALIZE** to enter the mission map for that stage.
- **Seasonal Events**: Look for the banner at the top. Active events (like Spring Blossom) grant **Bonus XP** for every mission completed.

### ⚔️ [FORGE] Real-Time Coding
*Where logic meets the metal.*
- Access this via the **Forge** icon in the bottom dock.
- Here you solve problems in our high-performance code editor.
- **Check** your logic, pass the tests, and claim your XP.

### 🏆 [ARENA] Global Rankings
*Measure your progress against the elite.*
- Visit the **Arena** to see the global leaderboard.
- XP and Streaks determine your standing. High-performance students are highlighted in neon.

### 🍱 [ELITE COMMAND] The Tactical Menu
*Secondary systems and specialized training.*
- Tap the **Menu/Hamburger** icon on the bottom right.
- **PYQs**: Access 2 years of real interview questions from top companies (like Zoho).
- **Lore & Puzzles**: Deep-dive into coding theory or solve quick logic puzzles to keep your mind sharp.
- **Training**: Specialized drills for competitive programming.

### 🦊 [PROFILE] Identity & Customization
*Your elite persona.*
- Tap the **Profile** icon to view your stats, streak, and total XP.
- **Animal Picker**: Click your avatar image to open the glassmorphic selector and switch your mascot instantly.
- **Share**: Use the sharing button to export your progress to social media.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Maintain your Streak!** Logging in and completing just one mission a day keeps your streak alive and triggers "Extreme Gaming" animations on your profile.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Responsive Design**: If you are on mobile, use the **Elite Command Drawer** for quick navigation. The dashboard has been optimized to ensure no content is hidden, even on smaller screens.

**Happy Coding, Student. Initialize your first mission now.** 🥇🚀🏆
